About us

Our Mission

































Buddy Chef was founded in Los Angeles by Juan Ruiz in 2020. Buddy Chef is a platform that connects health conscious individuals to local food growers and chefs in their area. At Buddy Chef we aim to provide every household with wholesome recipes, and connections with chefs and local food growers. We provide easy-to-follow recipes and information on where to find local, fresh, high-quality ingredients. We offer a variety of easy-to-follow recipes for every lifestyle and diet, from keto to plant-powered and beyond.

Our mission is to create a Los Angeles based network that focuses on healthy eating while supporting local farmers markets in the city of Los Angeles.
