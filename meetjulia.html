Julia Roberts

Julia specialices in




Julia Roberts is a farmer, author, speaker and consultant. Her area of expertise is in quick growing, high value annual vegetables for direct consumer market streams. His book, The Urban Farmer demonstrates organic intensive techniques with a focus on business and systems to streamline labour and production. She offers a new way to think about farming. One where quality of life and profitability coexist. Julias’ farm, Green City Acres is located in Ventura County, California and was established in 2010.

Find Julia at Lartchmonth Village farmers market (Friday and Sunday) 9am - 2pm
