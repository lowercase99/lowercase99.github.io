Avocado Toast

By chef Andres

Watch chef Andres making this dish
Avocado Recipes





Ingredients


  • ¼ Avocado
  • ¼ Teaspoon ground pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 slice whole-wheat bread, toasted
  • 1 large egg, fried
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon scallion, sliced (Optional)

Directions


  • Step 1: Combine avocado, pepper and garlic powder in a small bowl and gently mash
  • Step 2: Top toast with the avocado mixture and fried egg. Garnish with Sriracha and scallion, if desired.
