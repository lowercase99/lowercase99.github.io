Quinoa Salad

By chef Andres Olver

Watch chef Andres making this dish
Quinoa Recipes



































Ingredients


  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 8 ounces fresh cherry tomatoes
  • 1 large cucumber,
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • 5 ounces fresh spinach
  • 2 large ripe avocados
  • 1/4 of 1 bunch fresh cilantro

Directions


  • Step 1: Cook the quinoa according to package directions. Fluff and set aside to cool.
  • Step 2: Meanwhile, prep the dressing. Whisk the red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, oregano, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste), and 1/4 teaspoon pepper (or to taste) together in a small bowl. Slowly add in the olive oil into the vinegar mixture while whisking briskly. Whisk in the lemon juice. Pour into a jar and store in the fridge while prepping the vegetables.
